COMP 3104 DevOps teaches CI/CD, automation, and cloud integration.
The course covers Git, Docker, Kubernetes, and Jenkins.
Automation in DevOps reduces errors and improves efficiency.
DevOps for System Administration is a course that focuses on the principles and practices of DevOps.
Students will learn about continuous integration, continuous delivery, and various tools used for system administration in the DevOps environment.
The course aims to bridge the gap between development and operations, enhancing collaboration and automation in IT operations.
